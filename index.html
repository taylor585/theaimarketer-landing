faq

Questions, answered.

What exactly do I get from one run?

One brief produces a full marketing suite: Reddit-grounded ICP research, 90 hooks, 72 emails (four sequences), ad copy, static-image copy, video scripts, legal disclaimers and a VSL script — all generated in parallel and kept on-brand.

How long does a campaign take?

Typically ~15–20 minutes end-to-end. ICP research runs first; the moment it lands, the other seven generators fire at once, so the run finishes in the time of the slowest single step — not the sum of all of them.

Do I have to run the whole pipeline?

No. Every generator also works standalone — open just the Email Creator, 90 Hooks, Ad Copy or any other tool on its own whenever you only need one asset.

What is Terminal Rooms?

An interactive terminal inside the product, paired with Jarvis — a voice-and-text agent with persistent memory. It spawns real OS shells behind a safety gate. It's rolling out to Agency plans first.

How does it stay on-brand?

Your Knowledge Base, Quality Control examples and Master AI Rules are applied to every generator, so output matches your client's voice and compliance needs across the whole suite.
