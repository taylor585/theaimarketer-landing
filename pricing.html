questions

Good to know.

What's a "campaign launch"?

One full run of the 8-step auto-pipeline — ICP research plus all seven generators firing in parallel for a single client. Running individual tools on their own doesn't count against your launches.

What's the difference between manual and automated ICP?

On Starter you paste your own customer research to ground every asset. Studio and Agency run automated Reddit ICP research via Manus AI as the first pipeline step — no manual input needed.

What are Terminal Rooms?

A real, interactive terminal inside The AI Marketer — live OS shells over a WebSocket, paired with Jarvis, a voice-and-text agent with persistent memory. It runs on dedicated infrastructure, so it's exclusive to Agency.

Can I change plans later?

Yes — upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately and unlock the pipeline, image generation or Terminal Rooms on the spot.

What happens if I hit my launch limit?

Your standalone generators keep working. To run more full pipelines that month you can top up launches or move up a plan — Agency is unlimited.

Do annual plans save money?

Yes — annual billing is two months free versus paying monthly, on every plan.
